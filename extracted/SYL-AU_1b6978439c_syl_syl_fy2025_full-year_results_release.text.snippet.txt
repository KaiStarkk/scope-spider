Outlook & Summary  
Symal has a strong platform for FY26 and beyond with strong WIH and future pipeline across 
a number of  growing industries including infrastructure, power and renewables, data centres,  
utilities, defence and building and facilities . The recent acquisitions of Ascot Bins  and Locale 
Civil8 have expanded Symal’s exposure to the circular economy  and provided a recurring 
revenue stream in the utilities  sector .  
In FY26, Symal expects to deliver normalised  EBITDA of between $115  million and $125  
million9.
